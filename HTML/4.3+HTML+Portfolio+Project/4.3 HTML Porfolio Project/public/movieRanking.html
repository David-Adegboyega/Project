The Best Movies According to David

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Rookie

This series follows a police officer in the LAPD. I really enjoy the storyline and the way it is written.

Step Up 3

Step Up 3 is an energetic and entertaining dance movie with amazing choreography and thrilling performances. The chemistry between the dancers makes every scene exciting, and the international crew adds fresh, diverse styles to the dance battles.

Power Rangers

This is one movie I can't get tired of.
